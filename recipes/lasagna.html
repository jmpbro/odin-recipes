Lasagne Recipe

Cooked lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, then baked until golden and bubbly.

Cooked lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish featuring layers of wide pasta noodles alternated with a savory meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or seasoned ricotta cheese, and generous amounts of melted mozzarella and Parmesan. Baked to perfection, it comes out hot, bubbling, and golden on top, with rich flavors and a satisfying, hearty texture.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (or a mix)
  • Crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Diced tomatoes (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, basil, thyme)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic; sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, seasonings (salt, pepper, Italian herbs), and optional diced tomatoes.
  5. Simmer uncovered for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
